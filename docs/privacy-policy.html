Privacy Policy

Auto Standup Bot — Last updated: March 29, 2026

This policy describes how the Auto Standup Bot Forge app (“the App”) handles information when you use it with Atlassian Jira Cloud.

Who we are

The App is provided by the developer identified on the Atlassian Marketplace listing. For questions about this policy or your data, contact: piranaminullah@gmail.com.

What the App does

The App reads your Jira activity (within your permission scopes) and optional connected services you configure, then uses an AI service to generate standup-style summaries. It may post those summaries to messaging channels you configure (for example Slack or Microsoft Teams).

Information the App processes

  • Jira data: Issue metadata and activity needed to build your standup (for example issues assigned to you, summaries, status, comments, and changelog details as permitted by Jira API scopes).
  • Configuration you provide: Settings stored in Atlassian Forge app storage, such as timezone, schedule preferences, optional Slack/Teams webhook URLs, optional GitHub connection and filters, and custom prompt text.
  • Generated content: Standup text and related history records stored in app storage so you can review past outputs.
  • GitHub (optional): If you connect GitHub, the App uses your authorization to read commit and pull request information associated with your account, subject to the permissions you grant.

Third-party services

When you use features that rely on them, data may be sent to:

  • OpenAI — to generate standup text from the activity summaries the App prepares.
  • Slack or Microsoft Teams — when you configure webhooks, the App sends the standup message content to the URL you provide.
  • GitHub — when connected, to retrieve development activity.

Those services have their own terms and privacy policies.

Where data is stored

App configuration and generated standup history are stored using Atlassian Forge app storage associated with your Jira Cloud site installation, as described in Atlassian’s documentation for Forge apps.

Legal bases and use

We process this information to provide the App’s features you request (standup generation, scheduling, and delivery). We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your Jira content to train public AI models.

Retention

Data remains in Forge app storage until you remove it by changing settings, disconnecting integrations, or uninstalling the App, subject to Atlassian’s platform retention rules.

Your choices

  • Disconnect GitHub or remove webhooks in the App settings.
  • Disable auto-posting or uninstall the App from your site.
  • Contact us at the email above for access or deletion requests related to data we hold as the app vendor (Atlassian may also provide site-level tools).

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
